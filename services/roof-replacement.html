Roof Replacement

When repairs aren't enough, a full roof replacement is the smartest long-term investment. We handle every detail — from tear-off to final inspection — with zero hassle.

Why Replace?

When It's Time to
Start Fresh

There comes a point where patching isn't cost-effective anymore. If your roof is 20+ years old, has widespread damage, or requires repeated repairs, a full replacement delivers better value, stronger protection, and a dramatically improved home appearance.

How It Works

Our Replacement Process

A full roof replacement is a major project. Here's exactly what to expect when you work with Boost Roofing.

01

Free Inspection & Estimate

We assess your roof thoroughly and provide a transparent, detailed quote with no surprises.

02

Material Selection

Choose from a range of premium shingles, styles, and colors. We guide you to what performs best in Arizona's climate.

03

Tear-Off & Prep

We remove the existing roof, inspect the decking, and replace any damaged wood before installation begins.

04

Installation

Underlayment, flashing, ice & water shield (where applicable), and new shingles installed to manufacturer specs.

05

Cleanup

We use magnetic rollers and thorough cleanup to ensure no nails or debris remain in your yard or driveway.

06

Final Inspection & Warranty

A thorough walkthrough with you before we hand over the warranty documents — both workmanship and manufacturer.

FAQ

Replacement Questions

Roof replacement costs depend on roof size, pitch, material choice, and current condition. In the Phoenix metro area, most residential replacements range from $8,000”“$20,000+. We provide a detailed free estimate so you know exactly what to expect before signing anything.
Most residential roof replacements are completed in 1”“3 days. Larger homes or complex roof designs may take longer. We schedule work to minimize disruption and will give you a specific timeline upfront.
We offer a full range of asphalt shingles including 3-tab, architectural/dimensional, and premium impact-resistant options. We'll help you choose what performs best in Arizona's heat and monsoon conditions and fits your budget.
If your replacement is needed due to storm damage, hail, or other covered events, homeowner's insurance often covers the majority of the cost. We work with insurance adjusters regularly and can help you navigate the claims process.
