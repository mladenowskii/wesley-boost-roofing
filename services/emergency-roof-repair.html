FAQ

Emergency Repair Questions

Response times vary based on crew availability and conditions (we can't work in lightning). We prioritize emergencies and dispatch as fast as possible. In most cases we can reach you the same day. Call (480) 602-4277 immediately — don't fill out a form during an emergency.
We use heavy-duty commercial tarps, secured properly across the damaged area to prevent any additional water entry. This is a temporary measure to protect your home while the permanent repair is planned and scheduled.
Storm-related emergency repairs are typically covered under homeowner's insurance, including temporary tarping costs. We document everything thoroughly and can work directly with your insurance adjuster. File your claim as soon as possible — don't wait.
Stay safe — do not go on the roof yourself. Move valuables away from leaking areas. Place buckets under active drips. Take photos for insurance documentation. If there is structural collapse or safety risk, leave the area and call us and emergency services.
