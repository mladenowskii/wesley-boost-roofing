Preventive Care

Roof Inspection

A professional inspection catches small problems before they become expensive emergencies. Know exactly what condition your roof is in — with a detailed written report.

Why Inspect?

Your Roof Can't
Tell You What's Wrong

Most roof damage is invisible from the ground. Failing flashing, cracked underlayment, poor ventilation — these problems quietly worsen until you have a major leak or structural issue on your hands. An annual professional inspection is the single best thing you can do to extend your roof's life.

Our licensed inspectors go where you can't — checking every surface, penetration, and drainage point — and give you a clear written report with photos.

Inspection Scope

What Our Inspectors Check

Shingles & Surface

Missing, cracked, curling, or blistered shingles. Granule loss assessment to gauge remaining lifespan.

Flashing & Seals

All flashing around chimneys, skylights, vents, and valleys — the most common failure points.

Gutters & Drainage

Clogged or improperly pitched gutters cause water backup and fascia damage. We check it all.

Ventilation

Inadequate attic ventilation causes heat buildup that prematurely ages shingles and raises energy bills.

Structural Integrity

Sagging ridgelines, soft decking spots, and signs of structural movement are flagged immediately.

Attic & Interior

Moisture stains, mold, daylight penetration, and insulation condition checked from inside when accessible.
